# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove team impediments, and serve as a coach for the team and stakeholders about agile best practices.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers and help resolve team impediments
- Coach the team on agile principles and continuous improvement
- Foster transparency and psychological safety
- Support accurate estimation and sprint commitment

### Goals
- Enable sustainable team velocity and predictable delivery
- Encourage learning and adaptation through feedback loops
- Ensure process alignment and remove friction in delivery

### Typical Communication
- Daily with the delivery team
- Regular status with PM and Product Manager
- Proactively with stakeholders regarding blockers/escalations

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works closely with Developers, Product Managers, and Project Managers to ensure effective execution
- Often acts as a liaison to leadership regarding process improvement

---

## UX Designer/Researcher

### Role Summary
UX Designers/Researchers design, validate, and iterate on the user experience, ensuring solutions are user-centric and accessible.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design assets
- Gather and synthesize customer feedback into actionable insights
- Collaborate on backlog refinement and acceptance criteria for design requirements

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible, and delightful user experiences
- Ensure user feedback is continuously incorporated into the product
- Reduce support escalations tied to usability

### Typical Communication
- Regular feedback sessions with users and stakeholders
- Ongoing sync with Product Managers and Developers
- Share findings and recommendations through research reports and design reviews

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Partners with Product Manager to prioritize design and research
- Collaborates with Developers on implementation details and feasibility

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers plan and coordinate releases, ensuring readiness and communicating status to all stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Define the release plan and schedule
- Assess release readiness and coordinate pre-release checks
- Communicate release status, changes, and incidents
- Maintain the release calendar, cut notes, and retrospective actions

### Goals
- Ensure smooth, predictable releases with minimal disruption
- Align all teams around release expectations and responsibilities
- Facilitate fast incident response when required

### Typical Communication
- Coordinates with Developers, Project Managers, QA, and stakeholders before and after releases
- Responsible for release notes and crucial updates

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Close collaboration with DevOps Engineers for deployment and rollback procedures
- Supports QA in pre-release validation
- Keeps PM, stakeholders, and users informed

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers manage deployment pipelines, infrastructure as code, and operational health of production systems.

### Responsibilities
- Automate build, test, and deployment pipelines
- Monitor production systems for reliability and performance
- Maintain infrastructure as code and configuration management
- Collaborate in incident response and troubleshooting

### Goals
- Reduce deployment risk and cycle time
- Improve service uptime and scalability
- Enable self-serve deployment and environment setup for engineers

### Typical Communication
- Daily with Developers, QA, and Release Managers
- Incident reviews and knowledge sharing sessions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Enables Developers and QA to deliver and test efficiently
- Partners with Release Manager on deployment and versioning
- Provides operational transparency to PMs and incident teams
